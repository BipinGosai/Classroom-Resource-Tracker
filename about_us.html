ABOUT US

Classroom Resource Tracker is an application designed by the students of Kathmandu University. The purpose of the application is to track the availability and status of classroom resources. The application also allow teachers to book resources for specific date and time slots. It also helps to maintain clear record of maintenance history.

In an increasingly data-driven educational landscape, the need for effective tracking of resource utilization has become paramount. This application has developed a comprehensive system that enables educators to monitor and access student learning outcomes while optimizing classroom resources. This application provides real-time resource visibility, simplifies the booking process, and ensures timely maintenance, all of which contributes to smoother classroom operations, cost savings, and a more organized and reliable educational environment.
